The starting point

Every recurring block, every unexplained physical symptom, every limit you cannot seem to move past — has a root. It is not in the mind. It is in the body — still carrying the weight of a belief that no longer belongs to you.

Real change does not happen through understanding. It happens through feeling.

The method

Three phases · One session
01

Investigation

Before we work, I listen deeply. I use your Human Design as a map and your story as a thread — to find the precise knot generating the block, not the surface symptom. Lived hypersensitivity, intuition sharpened session by session, active listening.

02

Release

In a state of expanded awareness — you are present and conscious throughout — I guide your attention towards the sensations in the body where the block is held. You do not need to understand everything. You need to feel.

03

Integration

The limiting belief loses its charge. The body integrates what the mind had only half understood. What remains is a freer and more authentic version of you, one that does not ask permission to exist.

About

I am a hypnotherapist and I have worked for years with people carrying deep blocks — the kind the mind understands but the body cannot yet let go of.
